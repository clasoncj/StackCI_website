Bootstrap Your DevOps Practice


Infrastructure Audit

Let us audit your existing IT infrastucture and formulate a plan to best utilize your existing resources while assisting your transition cloud. StackCI has extensive experience with traditional enterprise environments in addition to private cloud, managed cloud, and various public cloud providers. We can support squeezing every last cent out of existing infrastructure, migrating as fast as possible to public clouds, and everything in-between.

DevOps and Cloud Readiness Workshop

Do you want to improve developer efficiencies, reduce time to market, and reduce costs but don't know where to start? Let StackCI evaluate your processes and procedures, existing infrastructure, software toolchains, and your applications. Our holistic approach will create a personalized digital transformation roadmap aligned with your long term business vision with an emphasis on short term, high payback items.

DevOps Toolchain Deployment and Configuration

Let StackCI's team of experts get your DevOps toolchain deployed and configured for your development teams. Ensure the toolchain is configured correctly from Day 1 and avoid major pitfalls which can lead to inefficiencies down the road. This service is typically the next step after our DevOps and Cloud Readiness Workshop but is also useful for teams who know what they want but lack experience or resources to properly deploy and configure the toolchain.
