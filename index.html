DevOps Toolchain Deployment

Let StackCI's team of experts get your DevOps toolchain deployed and configured for your development teams. Application Onboarding and Development


Lift and Shift

Many existing applications can be redeployed into a cloud environment and see benefits by integration into a DevOps pipeline. You will see immediate efficiency gains transitioning to an infrastructure as code model and implementing configuration management for your applications. We have experience developing and managing both legacy and cloud native applications allowing us to develop a cloud migration strategy tailored to your specific goals and needs.

Application Replatforming

Once your application is running in the cloud, the next step is make adjustments to better take advantage of the new platform. We can modify your applications to take advantage of services offered by the cloud provider, replace commercial components with open source alternatives and enable application modules to scale out so you can take advantage elastic nature of the cloud.

App Development and Refactoring

When you demand the ultimate in performance and scalability, rewriting or refactoring your application often is the best choice. Our team has experience with all aspects of scale out applications including, frontend, backend, and middleware. We also have domain expertise in the big data and realtime analytics space that we can apply towards your project. No project is too large or too small plus we can augment your team or complete the project independently.
